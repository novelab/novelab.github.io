REMOTE CONTROL OPERATION

The operation is a project led by the social media and digital agency Uzful. Through a web interface, the user can give an order to a robot that plays Little Big Planet 3 of PS4 in remote play via tablle Xperia Z3.

The installation was visible in the Sony chalet Christmas market Champs Elysees in Paris and broadcast live on the website http://operation-remotectrl.com/ so users can track in real time their own interractions with the robot .

The device

Using methods and rapid prototyping tools, we were able to design a robot in very short time. The device was designed and produced locally:
- Design and production monitoring by Uzful.
- Design of the robot, electronic and Arduino programming by Novelab with the help of Beatrice Lartigue for the overall design.
- Manufacture of the robot using the digital workshop (laser cutter, 3D printer, digital milling machine ...) of ICI Montreuil by Pierrick Verger.

Virtual Visit for Suez Environment

Thanks to the Oculus Rift, the experience allows a virtual visite of the wastewater treatment plant of "La Feyssine de Lyon". Using pictures spheres with full HD quality, the user finds himself in total immersion in defferent places of the station. Pop-up information appears when the user looks carrefuly at one part of the room, he can interact on some of these places in order to have additional visual or audio information or to change for an other location.

With this application, Suez Environnement provides a visit of it station in a pleasant, immersive and innovative way while eliminating the time and space constraints.

The application was presented during the "Salon des maires et des collectivités locales de Paris "(25 to 27 November, 2014) and at the "Salon Pollutec de Lyon" (2 to 5 December, 2014).
